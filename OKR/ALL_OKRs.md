# **6. HOW TO RUN THE AFRIXPORT OKR PROCESS**

## **Quarterly Rituals**

### **1. OKR Planning (Week 1)**

* CEO sets company OKRs
* Each leader drafts department OKRs
* Validate alignment across regions

### **2. Weekly Check-In (Every Monday)**

Leaders answer 3 questions:

1. What progress did you make last week?
2. What are you focusing on this week?
3. What roadblocks do you have?

### **3. Monthly Review**

* Score each Key Result (0.0 to 1.0)
* Identify progress blockers
* CEO gives direction

### **4. Quarterly Review**

* Review performance
* Adjust OKRs for next cycle
* Reward contributions based on weight
